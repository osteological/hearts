The Party

💃🎶🕺🎉

Get ready to dance the night away on the rhythmic waves of the Bay Mist! Our party, helmed by Maine’s finest live musicians and DJs, will beckon you on the dance floor for a night of non-stop fun.

👗👔👕👚

Dress to impress in your favorite cocktail threads that allow you to tear up the dance floor uninhibited. We encourage a splash of bright, cheerful hues to complement our maritime backdrop. The ship is enclosed and heated, so no need to dress for the ocean elements.

🍕🍩🍷🍻

To balance out the open bar (yes, the boat has a bar!), we’ll be serving pizza and donuts keep you fueled for an evening of revelry. We encourage you to eat dinner prior to your arrival, as this event is happening following a private dinner and ceremony attended by family.

The Schedule

The boat departs from Casco Bay terminals located at 56 Commercial Street and Maine State Pier. Information on directions and parking can be found on their website. The cruise will last a total of four hours, and we'll be making a single stop in the middle to drop off partygoers and pick up anyone who may have missed the first departure.
